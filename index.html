About Skeelers

Founded in Kottayam, Skeelers is more than just a club; it's a community built on a shared passion for roller skating. Our mission is to provide a safe, fun, and professional environment for skaters of all ages and skill levels to learn, grow, and connect.

From first-timers finding their balance to seasoned athletes competing at the state level, our certified coaches are here to guide you every step of the way.

Find Your Discipline

Speed Quad

Master speed and agility on traditional quad skates. Perfect for rink racing and building powerful fundamentals.

Speed Inline

Feel the rush of inline speed skating. This discipline focuses on power, technique, and endurance for track and road.

Alpine

Slalom skating on wheels. Weave through cones with precision and speed in this highly technical and exciting discipline.

Downhill

The ultimate adrenaline rush. Learn safety, form, and control for navigating hills and inclines at speed.

Roller Hockey

A fast-paced team sport that combines skating skills with stickhandling and strategy. Join our league!
