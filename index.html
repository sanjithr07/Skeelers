About Us

About Skeelers

We are a roller skating club dedicated to bringing people together through joy, freedom, and fitness. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned skater, Skeelers is your home on wheels.

Founded in Kottayam, Skeelers began with a dream: to make skating more than a sport — a lifestyle. From a small group of enthusiasts, we've grown into a vibrant community celebrating movement, friendship, and growth.

At Skeelers, we believe that skating isn't just about speed or tricks — it's about confidence, creativity, and discipline.

More About Skeelers

What We Do

We offer year-round training, bank track practice, road practice, workshops, and events for skaters of all ages and skill levels. From recreational skating to competitive training, Skeelers provides expert guidance in a supportive environment.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower individuals to embrace the spirit of skating — promoting health, happiness, and community through every stride.

Our Vision

To build a culture where roller skating becomes a lifestyle of expression, fitness, and unity, accessible to everyone.

Why Choose Skeelers

  • Inclusive community: Everyone is welcome, from first-time visitors to seasoned professionals.
  • Professional coaching: Learn from passionate, certified trainers.
  • Safe environment: Modern equipment and protective practices at every level.
  • Vibrant culture: Events, workshops, and skate fests that keep the spirit alive.

Let's roll together — because at Skeelers, the journey is always in motion.

Training

Training & Disciplines

A clear structure from your first session to competitive performance — across five skating disciplines.

Training Progression

Phase01

Foundation

Equipment familiarization, posture, balance, controlled rolling, and safe stopping techniques.

Phase02

Skill Development

Agility drills, speed control, directional transitions, and structured track navigation.

Phase03

Performance

Endurance training, speed drills, competitive simulation, and advanced movement coaching.

* Progression timelines depend on individual learning pace.
