About Our Club

Founded in 2010, Skeelers has been at the heart of the local skating community. Our mission is to promote roller sports as a fun and healthy activity for all ages and skill levels. We believe in building a supportive community where everyone can share their passion for skating.

Disciplines

Inline Skating

Experience the speed and agility of inline skating, from fitness to aggressive styles. This discipline is perfect for those who love a fast-paced workout and the thrill of executing precise maneuvers.

Quad Skating

Groove to the rhythm with classic quad skates, perfect for rink dancing and artistic skating. Quad skating offers a stable platform for expressive movements and creative dance routines.

Roller Hockey

Join our fast-paced roller hockey leagues for a thrilling team sport experience. It combines the speed of skating with the strategy and teamwork of hockey.

Alpine Skating

Challenge yourself with the adrenaline rush of downhill alpine skating. This discipline requires immense skill, courage, and precision as you navigate steep courses at high speeds.

Downhill

Master the art of speed and control in competitive downhill racing. This is the ultimate test of a skater's ability to handle extreme speeds and challenging road conditions.
