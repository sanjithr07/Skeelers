About Skeelers

We are a roller skating club dedicated to bringing people together through joy, freedom, and fitness. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned skater, Skeelers is your home on wheels.

Our Story

Founded in Kottayam, Skeelers began with a dream: to make skating more than a sport — a lifestyle. From a small group of enthusiasts, we've grown into a vibrant group celebrating movement, friendship, and growth.

At Skeelers, we believe that skating isn't just about speed or tricks — it's about confidence, creativity, and discipline.

Find Your Discipline

Speed Quad

Master speed and agility on traditional quad skates. Perfect for rink racing and building powerful fundamentals.

Speed Inline

Feel the rush of inline speed skating. This discipline focuses on power, technique, and endurance for track and road.

Alpine

Slalom skating on wheels. Weave through cones with precision and speed in this highly technical and exciting discipline.

Downhill

The ultimate adrenaline rush. Learn safety, form, and control for navigating hills and inclines at speed.

Roller Hockey

A fast-paced team sport that combines skating skills with stickhandling and strategy. Join our league!

More About Skeelers

What We Do

We offer year-round training, bank track practice, road practice, workshops, and events for skaters of all ages and skill levels.

From recreational skating and fitness sessions to competitive training and performance coaching, Skeelers provides expert guidance and a supportive environment that helps you glide beyond your limits. Our certified instructors focus on safety, technique, and fun — ensuring that every session is both rewarding and exhilarating.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower individuals to embrace the spirit of skating — promoting health, happiness, and community through every stride.

Our Vision

To build a culture where roller skating becomes a lifestyle of expression, fitness, and unity, accessible to everyone.

Why Choose Skeelers

  • Inclusive community: Everyone is welcome, from first-time visitors to seasoned professionals.
  • Professional coaching: Learn from passionate, certified trainers.
  • Safe environment: Modern equipment and protective practices at every level.
  • Vibrant culture: Events, workshops, and skate fests that keep the spirit alive.

Join us at Skeelers and rediscover the thrill of movement, the rhythm of wheels, and the freedom of the open floor.

Let's roll together — because at Skeelers, the journey is always in motion.
