Our Disciplines

Five worlds of skating, one club. Find your discipline and start your journey.

Speed Quad

Master speed and agility on traditional four-wheel quad skates. Perfect for rink racing, bank track events, and building a powerful foundational skating style that translates across all disciplines.

Track Racing Road Racing Beginner Friendly

Speed Inline

Feel the rush of inline speed skating. This discipline focuses on raw power, efficient stride technique, and endurance — built for road races, track events, and competitions at state and national level.

Track Racing Road Racing More Competitive

Alpine

Slalom skating on wheels. Weave through precisely spaced cones demanding balance, rhythm, and technical precision. Alpine is the most stylistic and artistic of all speed skating disciplines — deeply rewarding to master.

Road Full Gear Agility and Focus

Downhill

The ultimate adrenaline rush. Downhill training teaches safety-first form, aerodynamic body posture, and controlled braking so skaters can tackle steep inclines at speed — safely and confidently.

Road Speed Control Steep Declines

Roller Hockey

A fast-paced team sport that fuses skating skill with stickhandling, spatial awareness, and team strategy. Our hockey sessions are open to all levels — join our league and roll with the team!

Team Protective Gear Strategy & Speed
